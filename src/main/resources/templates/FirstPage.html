PROCEDURE FOR REFUND OF CAUTION MONEY AND LIBRARY DEPOSITS: The amount of the Caution Money and Library Deposits will be refunded when a student leaves the college or cancels his admission. A student is required to fill in the prescribed application form which can be had from the college counter and submit the same to the office duly filled in, dated, signed accompanied by the necessary deposit receipts, Identity Cards. The deposit not claimed within a period of one year will be forfeited. The amount of deposit will be refunded to the student after 15 days from date of the receipts of their application. The students who have not surrenderedtheir Identity Cards should surrender the same against the deposit refund.
